About This Project

The Student Food Support Dashboard is part of the Healthy Food Deficit Project, developed by BMCC CIS395 student. This platform helps manage essential resources such as students, volunteers, meals, and preparation logs to support food-insecure students across campus. The goal is to streamline food distribution and ensure no student goes hungry.
